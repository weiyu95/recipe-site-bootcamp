Crispy Rosemary Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 chicken thighs
  • 6 small red potatoes, quartered
  • ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil, or as needed
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 ½ teaspoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 ½ teaspoons garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Step 2: Place chicken and potatoes into a large bowl. Pour olive oil over them, and stir to coat. Scatter the chicken and potato pieces in a large baking dish, or cookie sheet with sides. Sprinkle with rosemary, oregano, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
  3. Step 3: Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven, uncovered. Baste during the last 15 minutes for extra crispness.
